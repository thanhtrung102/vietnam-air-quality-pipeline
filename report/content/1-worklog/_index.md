This worklog records how the **Vietnam Air Quality pipeline** was built during the internship. Unlike a
course-style log of AWS training modules, this was a single hands-on **build project**, so each entry
describes the engineering actually done — every item below traces to real commits in the
[project repository](https://github.com/thanhtrung102/vietnam-air-quality-pipeline) (205 commits between
**25 March and 2 June 2026**).

Work happened in five focused sprints: an intensive build phase in late March / early April that took
the pipeline end-to-end, followed by a hardening, reproducibility, and reporting phase in late May /
early June. Dates below are the actual working days from the commit history.
